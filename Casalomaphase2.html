An introduction to Casa Loma

Casa Loma is a historic castle that is located in Toronto, Ontario, it was built in 1914 and is now used as an event venue for weddings and other events. In modern times, over 500,000 tourists visit the history riddled castle and is now one of the most popular tourist locations in Toronto. In the year 2016, Casa Loma was named the best event venue in the entire continent of North America, beating out other famous event venues. Below is a picture of a wedding that took place at Casa Loma. The cost of the average wedding is $25,000 during the peak months of the year such as May - August, the wait list often exceeds multiple years due to the high demand of the venue. Casa Loma also does yearly events and since halloween is around the corner, they are currently selling tickets to their halloween themed events. Casa Loma is widely adored by historians and tourists around the globe.
