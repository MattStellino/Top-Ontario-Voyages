Ontario Science Centre

The Ontario Science Centre is a great place to spend your day in Toronto. They have tons of different interactive exhibits for people of all ages. There is also always an IMAX movie playing in their massive OMNIMAX theatre so you can sit back relax and enjoy the show while learning more about the planet.
