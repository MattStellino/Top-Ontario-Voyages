Algonquin Park

Algonquin park is located over 200km North of both Toronto and West of Ottawa. The park spans just over 7600 square kilometers and is home to various wild animals and numerous lakes and rivers throughout. Algonquin offers a variety of things from beautiful park scenery and various recreational activities to cabin rentals and fun park programs.
