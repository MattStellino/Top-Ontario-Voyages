A Question of Truth Test controversials theories including gender, culture, and race.

Only available for kids 8 and under and their guardians.
Space Hall Showcasing different things in our solar system, including meteorites, blackholes and more!
The Planetarium The only public planetarium in the area, sit and enjoy a journey through our solar system and discover more about our universe. For all ages.
The HotZone The place to ask all your burning questions and debate the answers.
The Bruce Poon Tip Living Earth Hall A natural exhibit that allows you to walk through the TELUS rainforest and scout for different animals, explore caves and experience the beauty of coral reefs.
Science Arcade A favorite for everyone of all ages.
