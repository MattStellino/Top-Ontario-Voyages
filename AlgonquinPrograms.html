Programs and Events

Group Education Programs:

Algonquin has many different education programs for both youth and adults that will allow you to explore Algonquins cultural and natural history. For a full list of their group programs you can visit their website below.
Education Programs

Algonquin Workshops:

The Algonuin workshops are provided by The Friends of Algonquin Park organization. They aim to to share their expertise and skills in a load of different recreational and historical subjects. Although the workshops are only opens to members of TFAP you may purchase individual memberships. Learn more info on their website.
Algonquin Workshops

Summer Interpretive Programs:

The various interpretive summer activities Algonquin has are perfect for all ages. They have Algonquin for kids which is interactive daily programs for your children(ages 5-12) involving all sorts of games, stories and other activities. They also have evening events such as holiday themed gatherings or informational meetings on anything from wild life to hiking the trails around the park.
