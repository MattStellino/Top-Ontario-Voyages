Camping and Lodging

Algonquin park offers a wide array of choices for how you want to spend your nights. You could rent a cabin or lodge for your stay or get your own spot on the campground near others who have also come to enjoy the beautiful sights. You may also bring your own RV or camping trailer there is plently of spots to plug in. Here is a list of the lakes and campground you are able to choose from for your trip to Algonquin, each spot has their own collection of camp spots and cabins/lodges to choose from.

  • Canisbay Lake
  • Kearney Lake
  • Kiosk
  • Lake of Two Rivers
  • Mew Lake
  • Pog Lake
  • Raccoon Lake
  • Rock Lake
  • Tea Lake
  • Whitefish Group Campground
