Introduction to Wonderland

Canada's Wonderland is the biggest amusement park in Canada and is one of the most iconic amusements parks in the world. Wonderland is ranked the second amusement park in the world in total roller coasters to which they currently have 17, they are behind Six Flags Magic Mountain. Wonderland does yearly events such as halloween haunt and winterfest to keep up with the yearly festivities. Wonderland opened in 1981 in Vaughan, Ontario, upon opening Wonderland was quickly filled with curious guests and was adored the Canadians. The entire park is situated on 300 acres of land with over 200 attractions and growing every year, they have a ride for every type of thrill seeker! Wonderland also has a water park section that has a plethora of rides for all ages, this part of the park is called Splash Works!

    Ride Facts

    Out of the entire catalog of rides in the park, leviathan is the fastest and tallest. It also holds this feat in all of Canada. Drop tower holds the feat as the fastest ride in the park that is not a roller coaster, with a staggering 100 km per hour speed. In Yukon Striker's opening year, the ride was used over 1.3 million times! It is now among the most popular rides in the park three years after its release. The longest and shortest roller coasters in the park share a 5000 feet difference, these two rides are named leviathan and taxi jam.
